Erbil, Iraq

Erbil is Iraqi Kurdistan's capital. Iraq and Afghanistan are among the top source countries for asylum seekers in British Columbia, according to aid organizations who work in B.C. Others include Honduras, Colombia, Guatemala, Burundi and Nigeria.
